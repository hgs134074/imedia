ABOUT

Eco Festival started back in 2010, at first it was just simply an idea thrown forward at a meal between five friends. But through hard work and three years of planning meant they could finally start their dream in the summer of 2010. The first year was a major success and since then it has rised in popularity every year. Bringing in better known artists and people from around the world who are wanting to visit for the festival.

How are we different?

Our festival is like no other. We feel very strongly about nature so we put this as our first priority. Every feature you see is completely priceless, the stage LED lights are p[owered by solar energy. Every camp has a camp bin room to prevent litter being left around.

This shows that we are a very special festival and it would be a mistake not to visit.
